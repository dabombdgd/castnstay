Our Accommodations

At Cast & Stay, we offer comfortable lodging options to suit every need. Our rooms are designed to provide a relaxing retreat after a day of fishing or exploring the waterfront.

Room Types

  • Waterfront Suites: Spacious rooms with panoramic lake views
  • Cozy Cabins: Rustic charm with modern amenities
  • Family Lodges: Perfect for larger groups or extended stays
  • Angler's Quarters: Convenient accommodations for fishing enthusiasts

Amenities

All our lodging options come equipped with:

  • Comfortable beds with premium linens
  • Private bathrooms with complimentary toiletries
  • Mini-fridges and coffee makers
  • Free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs
  • Outdoor seating areas to enjoy the scenery
